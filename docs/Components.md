## Interactive States (Buttons & Inputs)

Default
Normal styling and opacity

Hover
Smooth transition with slight elevation or brightness increase

Active / Pressed
Scale down slightly (e.g., 0.98) or remove shadow

Loading
Show a smooth inline spinner, disable clicking, keep width fixed

Disabled
50% opacity, no pointer events, no hover animation

---

## Feedback & Notifications (Toasts / Snackbars)

Success Toast
Green accent border/icon, auto-dismiss after 3000ms

Error Toast
Red accent border/icon, persistent until dismissed or 5000ms

Placement
Bottom-right on Desktop, Bottom-center on Mobile

---

## Data Lists & Tables

Row Items
Comfortable padding (16px vertical)

Clear actions on the right side

Pagination & Filters
Always visible at the top/bottom of data lists
Provide quick filters for common status checks
